How it works

This is a food delivery service specifically designed for vegetarians. We have partnered with 50+ restaurants in the GTA to offer you a variety of dish choices. Just download our app from the app store and order something and one of our runners will come running to you! We started this company because we want to help our vegan friends find a place where they can easily order food without checking twice. Everything they need right in their phone. From choosing the meal to delivery. Everything have been designed for you alone. Enjoy being a vegan in the 20th century. This is tomorrow's world designed for you alone.

  • 1. Order

    Download our VFoodRun app to order from a selection of restaurants

  • 2. Runner

    Our runners living nearby get the request and set out to deliver your food!

  • 3. Received

    You get your food and the runners get payed for the task by VFoodRun
